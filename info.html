Taejune Youn



Industrial Designer, based in Seoul.
2015-present

—
As a designer, I pay attention to a variety of expressions, from everyday experiences like red brick row houses and signage found while walking down the street to unfamiliar stories from different cultural backgrounds.

My works include stories from the past to those that envision the distant future. The collected narratives serve as constant hints for solving immediate and challenging problems.

In my portfolio, you will find traces of stories gathered and reflections on applying hints to create more diverse and comfortable environments.

Beyond just the work itself, I am exploring new paths through conversations with people in various cultures, seeking new avenues that I have not experienced before.
