I am a product designer specializing in consumer electronics and advanced design, with a degree in Industrial Design from Kookmin University.

I have collaborated on forward-thinking projects with Samsung Electronics and Hyundai Motors, gaining hands-on experience in innovative design solutions. My work includes participating in four projects under the Samsung Design Membership, as well as a design engineering project with Hyundai Motors and Hongik University.

Additionally, my passion for speculative design led to an individual exhibition where I explored conceptual ideas that push the boundaries of conventional design.
